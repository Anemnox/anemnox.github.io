Forecasting Floats in Turbulence

A Machine Learning Model in forecasting floats in the Atlantic Ocean as part of the DARPA's Forecasting Floats in Turbulence challenge in the Autumn of 2021. The Model was a modified LSTM model that took in data from floats provided by Sofar and GRIB data provided by the GFS-Wave model.
